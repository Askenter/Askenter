### What I work on

**GPU parallel reinforcement learning.** My thesis at Imperial built FlyBy, one of the fastest GPU parallel RL environments for training aerial agents, with thousands of episodes running at once on a single device. I am now exploring where RL simulation and training actually bottleneck robotics and embodied AI teams.

**Applied AI in the wild.** Through Aevori I deliver real systems for clients across Greece and the EU, including a civil service chatbot for the Greek public sector.
